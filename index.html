Privacy Policy for Neo Connect

Last updated: 2026-03-12

Neo Connect is a client application designed to establish secure network connections to private servers using credentials provided by the user.

Data Collection

Neo Connect does not collect personal data from users.

The application does not track users, create analytics profiles, or sell personal information.

Connection credentials and configuration are stored locally on the user’s device and are not transmitted to the developer.

Network Processing

Neo Connect establishes encrypted connections between the user’s device and servers configured by the user.

All network processing occurs locally on the device. The developer does not receive, store, or analyze connection logs or traffic data on external servers.

Permissions

The application may request the following permissions:

  • Internet – required to establish network connections.
  • VPN Service – used to route device traffic through the configured secure connection.
  • Foreground Service – required to maintain the secure connection while the app runs.

These permissions are used strictly for the core functionality of the application.

Third-Party Services

Neo Connect does not share personal data with third parties. The application does not include advertising SDKs or analytics trackers.

Security

Connection credentials and configuration remain on the user’s device. No sensitive information is transmitted to developer-controlled servers.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future to reflect changes in the application or legal requirements. Any updates will be published on this page.
